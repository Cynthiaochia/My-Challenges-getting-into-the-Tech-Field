MY CHALLENGES

But at the same time I try to push myself forward by not forgetting why I got into it the first place. I remind myself of my purpose and where I want to be in years to come and so I thrive harder.

I know that if I eventually achieve this ofcourse I would be working from home which would give us the whole time we need but it hurts that I spend more time at work and on my studies.
